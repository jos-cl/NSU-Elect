NSUElect is the election platform for the Nortec Student Union and class representative system. It enforces paid-up eligibility, commission-led election management, secret ballots, real-time leaderboards, and deployable Firebase infrastructure with local emulators for testing.

Northern Technical College

System Scope

Built around Articles 11, 13, 15, and related constitutional procedures.

Student Elections

Students log in with a verified student number, vote once per position, apply for nomination, and track leaders in real time.

Independent Commission

The Electoral Commission schedules elections, processes nominations, disqualifies candidates, declares unopposed candidates, and finalizes winners.

Oversight and Analytics

SAO oversight, participation reporting, audit trails, and academic-term scheduling checks are built into the backend flow.

Core Pages

Frontend pages mapped to the main user roles.

Student Dashboard

Election state, leaders, voting status, and past presidents.

Commission Dashboard

Scheduling, approvals, disqualifications, and declarations.

SAO Dashboard

Commission appointments, reporting, and student imports.
NSUElect runs on Firebase Hosting, Firestore, Authentication, Cloud Functions, and the Local Emulator Suite.
